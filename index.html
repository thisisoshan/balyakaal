WELCOME TO BALYAKAAL

Balyakaal is a small pre-school located at Thapa Gaun, Kathmandu. As the name itself suggests, we focus on the "childhood" of our students, looking for ways to make it memorable and fruitful. Although we're at our most initial phase, our objectives and plans for the future are so compelling that you won't need a history to judge us on.

And again, welcome to BALYAKAAL.

A Little about us

At Balyakaal, we look for ways to innovate our teaching methods so that the students get best out of it. Besides, one of our prime concerns is the student's overall growth. As classes progress on, we try our best to see what the student's individual interests are so that we can help them work on them. So we want to tackle this problem by identifying those core interests from childhood.
